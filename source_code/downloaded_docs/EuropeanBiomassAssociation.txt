     <p>
      The
      <b>
       European Biomass Association
      </b>
      (AEBIOM, from the official
      <a href="/wiki/French_language" title="French language">
       French
      </a>
      name
      <i>
       Association Européenne pour la Biomasse
      </i>
      ) is a European trade association opens to national
      <a href="/wiki/Biomass" title="Biomass">
       biomass
      </a>
      associations and
      <a href="/wiki/Bioenergy" title="Bioenergy">
       bioenergy
      </a>
      companies active in
      <a href="/wiki/Europe" title="Europe">
       Europe
      </a>
      . AEBIOM was founded in 1990 and aims to promote biomass production and application throughout Europe.
     </p>
     <p>
      AEBIOM is the umbrella organisation of the European Pellet Council (EPC),
      <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1">
       <a href="#cite_note-1">
        [1]
       </a>
      </sup>
      the European Industry of Pellet Suppliers (EIPS) and the International Biomass Torrefaction Council (IBTC).
      <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2">
       <a href="#cite_note-2">
        [2]
       </a>
      </sup>
     </p>
