   <h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">
    List of least carbon efficient power stations
   </h1>
   <div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">

    <div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en">
     <p>
      This is a
      <b>
       list of least carbon efficient power stations
      </b>
      in selected countries. Lists were created by the
      <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/WWF_(conservation_organization)" title="WWF (conservation organization)">
       WWF
      </a>
      and lists the most polluting
      <a href="/wiki/Power_station" title="Power station">
       power stations
      </a>
      in terms of the level of
      <a href="/wiki/Carbon_dioxide" title="Carbon dioxide">
       carbon dioxide
      </a>
      produced per unit of electricity generated. In 2005 WWF created list of power stations from 30 industrialised countries, also list for
      <a href="/wiki/European_Union" title="European Union">
       EU
      </a>
      ,
      <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1">
       <a href="#cite_note-1">
        [1]
       </a>
      </sup>
      in 2007 WWF published updated EU list. In 2009
      <a href="/wiki/European_Commission" title="European Commission">
       European Commission
      </a>
      list with absolute emissions only, also in 2014
      <a href="/wiki/Climate_Action_Network" title="Climate Action Network">
       Climate Action Network
      </a>
      Europe, WWF,
      <a href="/wiki/European_Environmental_Bureau" title="European Environmental Bureau">
       European Environmental Bureau
      </a>
      ,
      <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Health_and_Environment_Alliance&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Health and Environment Alliance (page does not exist)">
       Health and Environment Alliance
      </a>
      and
      <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Climate_Alliance_Germany&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Climate Alliance Germany (page does not exist)">
       Climate Alliance Germany
      </a>
      .
     </p>
