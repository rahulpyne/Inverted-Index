      </span>
      <span class="mw-editsection">
       <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
        [
       </span>
       <a href="/w/index.php?title=Renewable_energy_in_Thailand&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Solar energy">

     </h2>
     <div class="hatnote" role="note">
      Main article:
      <a href="/wiki/Solar_power_in_Thailand" title="Solar power in Thailand">
       Solar power in Thailand
      </a>
     </div>
     <p>
      Solar power in Thailand has been expanding since in 2011. Thailand was targeted to reach 55 MW by 2011.
      <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1">
       <a href="#cite_note-1">
        [1]
       </a>
      </sup>
     </p>
     <h2>
      <span class="mw-headline" id="Wind_energy">
       Wind energy
      </span>
      <span class="mw-editsection">
       <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
        [
       </span>
       <a href="/w/index.php?title=Renewable_energy_in_Thailand&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Wind energy">
        edit
       </a>
       <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
        ]
       </span>
      </span>
     </h2>
     <div class="hatnote" role="note">
      Main article:
      <a href="/wiki/Wind_power_in_Thailand" title="Wind power in Thailand">
       Wind power in Thailand
      </a>
     </div>
     <p>
      Wind power in Thailand amounts to an installed production capacity of 223
      <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Megawatt" title="Megawatt">
       MW
      </a>
      as of the end of 2013.
      <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2">
       <a href="#cite_note-2">
        [2]
       </a>
      </sup>
      Installed capacity was 112 MW by the end of 2012, with 111 MW added in 2013. This ranks Thailand
      <a href="/wiki/Wind_power_by_country" title="Wind power by country">
       34th in the world
      </a>
      by installed capacity.
     </p>
