     <p>
      The
      <b>
       Department of the Environment, Water, Heritage and the Arts
      </b>
      was an
      <a href="/wiki/Australia" title="Australia">
       Australian
      </a>
      <a href="/wiki/Government_of_Australia" title="Government of Australia">
       Government
      </a>
      department that existed between December 2007 and September 2010.
     </p>
     <h2>
      <span class="mw-headline" id="Scope">
       Scope
      </span>
      <span class="mw-editsection">
       <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
        [
       </span>
       <a href="/w/index.php?title=Department_of_the_Environment,_Water,_Heritage_and_the_Arts&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Scope">
        edit
       </a>
       <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
        ]
       </span>
      </span>
     </h2>
     <p>
      Information about the department's functions and/or government funding allocation could be found in the
      <a href="/wiki/Machinery_of_government#Australian_Government_Administrative_Arrangement_Orders" title="Machinery of government">
       Administrative Arrangements Orders
      </a>
      , the annual Portfolio Budget Statements and in the Department's annual reports.
     </p>
     <p>
      According to the Administrative Arrangements Order (AAO) made at the Department's establishment, the Department dealt with:
      <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-NAA_CA_9187_1-1">
       <a href="#cite_note-NAA_CA_9187-1">
        [1]
       </a>
      </sup>
     </p>
     <ul>
      <li>
       Environment protection and conservation of biodiversity
      </li>
      <li>
       Air quality
      </li>
      <li>
       National fuel quality standards
      </li>
      <li>
       Land contamination
      </li>
      <li>
       Meteorology
      </li>
      <li>
       Administration of the Australian Antarctic Territory, and the Territory of Heard Island and McDonald Islands
      </li>
      <li>
       Natural, built and movable cultural heritage
      </li>
      <li>
       Environmental research
      </li>
      <li>
       Water policy and resources
      </li>
      <li>
       Cultural affairs, including support for the arts
      </li>
      <li>
       Ionospheric prediction
      </li>
      <li>
       Community and household renewable energy programs
      </li>
     </ul>
