    <div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en">
     <p>
      <i>
       <b>
        Applied Energy
       </b>
      </i>
      is a
      <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Peer-reviewed" title="Peer-reviewed">
       peer-reviewed
      </a>
      <a href="/wiki/Academic_journal" title="Academic journal">
       academic journal
      </a>
      covering research on
      <a href="/wiki/Energy_engineering" title="Energy engineering">
       energy engineering
      </a>
      that was established in 1975. It is published by
      <a href="/wiki/Elsevier" title="Elsevier">
       Elsevier
      </a>
      and the
      <a href="/wiki/Editor-in-chief" title="Editor-in-chief">
       editor-in-chief
      </a>
      is Jinyue Yan. According to the
      <i>
       <a href="/wiki/Journal_Citation_Reports" title="Journal Citation Reports">
        Journal Citation Reports
       </a>
      </i>
      , the journal has a 2014
      <a href="/wiki/Impact_factor" title="Impact factor">
       impact factor
      </a>
      of 5.613, ranking it 9th out of 89 journals in the category "Energy &amp; Fuels" and 6th out of 135 journals in "engineering, chemical".
      <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1">
       <a href="#cite_note-1">
        [1]
       </a>
      </sup>
     </p>
