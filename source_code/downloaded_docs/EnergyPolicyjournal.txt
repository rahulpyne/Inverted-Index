     <p>
      <i>
       <b>
        Energy Policy
       </b>
      </i>
      is a monthly
      <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Peer-reviewed" title="Peer-reviewed">
       peer-reviewed
      </a>
      <a href="/wiki/Academic_journal" title="Academic journal">
       academic journal
      </a>
      covering research on
      <a href="/wiki/Energy_policy" title="Energy policy">
       energy policy
      </a>
      and
      <a href="/wiki/Energy_supply" title="Energy supply">
       energy supply
      </a>
      . It is published by
      <a href="/wiki/Elsevier" title="Elsevier">
       Elsevier
      </a>
      . According to the
      <i>
       <a href="/wiki/Journal_Citation_Reports" title="Journal Citation Reports">
        Journal Citation Reports
       </a>
      </i>
      , the journal has a 2013
      <a href="/wiki/Impact_factor" title="Impact factor">
       impact factor
      </a>
      of 2.696.
      <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-WoS_1-0">
       <a href="#cite_note-WoS-1">
        [1]
       </a>
      </sup>
