     <p>
      <b>
       COGEN Europe
      </b>
      is a European
      <a href="/wiki/Advocacy_group" title="Advocacy group">
       advocacy group
      </a>
      based in
      <a href="/wiki/Belgium" title="Belgium">
       Belgium
      </a>
      that promotes the practice of
      <a href="/wiki/Cogeneration" title="Cogeneration">
       cogeneration
      </a>
      in energy production. The group acts as a liaison between its member companies and
      <a href="/wiki/European_Union" title="European Union">
       European Union
      </a>
      energy organizations and committees.
      <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1">
       <a href="#cite_note-1">
        [1]
       </a>
      </sup>
      <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2">
       <a href="#cite_note-2">
        [2]
       </a>
      </sup>
      <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3">
       <a href="#cite_note-3">
        [3]
       </a>
      </sup>
     </p>
     <p>
      The managing director of COGEN Europe is also the coordinator of the
      <a class="external text" href="http://enefield.eu/" rel="nofollow">
       ene.field project
      </a>
      .
      <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4">
       <a href="#cite_note-4">
        [4]
       </a>
      </sup>
     </p>
