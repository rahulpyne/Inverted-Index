     <p>
      <b>
       Fleet vehicles
      </b>
      are groups of
      <a href="/wiki/Motor_vehicle" title="Motor vehicle">
       motor vehicles
      </a>
      owned or leased by a business, government agency or other organization rather than by an individual or family. Typical examples are vehicles operated by
      <a href="/wiki/Car_rental" title="Car rental">
       car rental companies
      </a>
      ,
      <a href="/wiki/Taxicab" title="Taxicab">
       taxicab companies
      </a>
      ,
      <a href="/wiki/Public_utility" title="Public utility">
       public utilities
      </a>
      , public bus companies, and police departments. In addition, many businesses purchase or lease fleet vehicles to deliver
      <a href="/wiki/Good_(economics)" title="Good (economics)">
       goods
      </a>
      to customers, or for sales representatives to travel to clients.
     </p>
     <h2>
      <span class="mw-headline" id="Federal_vehicle_fleet">
       Federal vehicle fleet
      </span>
      <span class="mw-editsection">
       <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
        [
       </span>
       <a href="/w/index.php?title=Fleet_vehicle&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Federal vehicle fleet">
        edit
       </a>
       <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
        ]
       </span>
      </span>
     </h2>
     <div class="hatnote" role="note">
      Further information:
      <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Presidential_State_Car_(United_States)" title="Presidential State Car (United States)">
       Presidential State Car (United States)
      </a>
      and
      <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Federal_vehicle_fleet" title="Federal vehicle fleet">
       Federal vehicle fleet
      </a>
     </div>
     <p>
      In the United States, Federal Vehicle fleets refers to the
      <a href="/wiki/Federal_government_of_the_United_States" title="Federal government of the United States">
       federal government
      </a>
      's vehicles.
     </p>
     <p>
      <br/>
      Fleet leasing in the UK is very much the same as in the USA, business van leasing is popular among the construction industry as a valuable asset. Fleet leasing is popular with much larger businesses with the ability to get great deals and discounts on multi leasing vehicles.
     </p>
