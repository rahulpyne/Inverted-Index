     <p>
      <b>
       Marorka
      </b>
      is a company which specializes in
      <a href="/wiki/Marine_energy_management" title="Marine energy management">
       marine energy management
      </a>
      . Marorka’s head office is in
      <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Reykjavik" title="Reykjavik">
       Reykjavik
      </a>
      ,
      <a href="/wiki/Iceland" title="Iceland">
       Iceland
      </a>
      along with its
      <a href="/wiki/Server_(computing)" title="Server (computing)">
       servers
      </a>
      and data storage infrastructure are supplied with electricity generated using
      <a href="/wiki/100%25_renewable_energy" title="100% renewable energy">
       100% renewable energy
      </a>
      resources –
      <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Geothermal" title="Geothermal">
       geothermal
      </a>
      and
      <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Hydroelectric" title="Hydroelectric">
       hydroelectric
      </a>
      . Marorka has international offices in
      <a href="/wiki/Copenhagen" title="Copenhagen">
       Copenhagen
      </a>
      , DK,
      <a href="/wiki/Singapore" title="Singapore">
       Singapore
      </a>
      , SG,
      <a href="/wiki/Athens" title="Athens">
       Athens
      </a>
      , GR and
      <a href="/wiki/Dubai" title="Dubai">
       Dubai
      </a>
      , AE. Marorka's mission is to deliver products and services to vessel owners and operators to save fuel, increase profitability and reduce harmful emissions.
      <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-mar_1-0">
       <a href="#cite_note-mar-1">
        [1]
       </a>
      </sup>
      The company was founded in June 2002 and resulted from the PhD thesis of Jón Ágúst Thorsteinsson, Entrepreneur and founder.
     </p>
     <p>
      <b>
       Marorka
      </b>
      has developed reliable, automated, on-board and online
      <a href="/wiki/Energy_management" title="Energy management">
       energy management
      </a>
      systems for the international
      <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Shipping_industry" title="Shipping industry">
       shipping industry
      </a>
      . Marorka’s products and services enable vessel operators to optimize fuel consumption by maximizing the
      <a href="/wiki/Energy_efficiency_in_transportation" title="Energy efficiency in transportation">
       energy efficiency
      </a>
      of their vessels through the implementation of real-time monitoring and decision support, which are essential components of any operational optimization process. Marorka’s commitment towards energy efficiency goes beyond the technological dimension – ships’ officers play a significant role in increasing energy efficiency and reducing emissions.
     </p>

     <p>
      Marorka's field of work:
     </p>
     <ul>
      <li>
       consultancy for vessel building
      </li>
      <li>
       operational analysis of ocean vessels
      </li>
      <li>
       development of Marorka Onboard (energy management system), the
       <a href="/wiki/Marine_energy_management" title="Marine energy management">
        marine energy management system
       </a>
      </li>
      <li>
       development of Marorka Online and Marorka Online SEEMP
      </li>
     </ul>
     <p>
      Marorka’s systems have been installed on board vessels of various types and sizes;
      <a href="/wiki/Fishing_vessel" title="Fishing vessel">
       Fishing vessels
      </a>
      ,
      <a href="/wiki/Cargo_ship" title="Cargo ship">
       Cargo vessels
      </a>
      ,
      <a href="/wiki/Bulk_carrier" title="Bulk carrier">
       Bulk carriers
      </a>
      ,
      <a href="/wiki/LNG_carrier" title="LNG carrier">
       LNG carriers
      </a>
      ,
      <a href="/wiki/Cruise_ship" title="Cruise ship">
       Cruise ships
      </a>
      and
      <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Research_Vessel" title="Research Vessel">
       Research vessels
      </a>
      .
     </p>
     <p>
      In 2008, Marorka received the
      <a href="/wiki/Nordic_Council_Environment_Prize" title="Nordic Council Environment Prize">
       Nordic Council Environment Prize
      </a>
      . 37 companies were nominated for the award.
      <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-mar_1-1">
       <a href="#cite_note-mar-1">
        [1]
       </a>
      </sup>
     </p>
     <p>
      Marorka has implemented a
      <a href="/wiki/Quality_management_system" title="Quality management system">
       quality management system
      </a>
      built on the
      <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/ISO_9001:2008" title="ISO 9001:2008">
       ISO 9001:2008
      </a>
      standard, certified by
      <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Det_Norske_Veritas" title="Det Norske Veritas">
       Det Norske Veritas
      </a>
      .
     </p>
