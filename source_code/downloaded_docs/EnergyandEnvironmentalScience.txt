     <p>
      <i>
       <b>
        Energy and Environmental Science
       </b>
      </i>
      is a monthly
      <a href="/wiki/Peer_review" title="Peer review">
       peer-reviewed
      </a>
      <a href="/wiki/Scientific_journal" title="Scientific journal">
       scientific journal
      </a>
      publishing
      <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Original_research" title="Original research">
       original (primary) research
      </a>
      and
      <a href="/wiki/Literature_review" title="Literature review">
       review articles
      </a>
      . The journal covers work of an interdisciplinary nature in the biochemical and biophysical sciences and chemical engineering disciplines.
      <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1">
       <a href="#cite_note-1">
        [1]
       </a>
      </sup>
      <i>
       Energy &amp; Environmental Science
      </i>
      is published by the
      <a href="/wiki/Royal_Society_of_Chemistry" title="Royal Society of Chemistry">
       Royal Society of Chemistry
      </a>
      and the
      <a href="/wiki/Editor-in-chief" title="Editor-in-chief">
       editor-in-chief
      </a>
      is Nathan Lewis (
      <a href="/wiki/California_Institute_of_Technology" title="California Institute of Technology">
       California Institute of Technology
      </a>
      ).
     </p>
     <p>
      According to the
      <i>
       <a href="/wiki/Journal_Citation_Reports" title="Journal Citation Reports">
        Journal Citation Reports
       </a>
      </i>
      , the journal has a 2015
      <a href="/wiki/Impact_factor" title="Impact factor">
       impact factor
      </a>
      of 25.427, ranking it 4th out of 163 journals in the category "Chemistry, Multidisciplinary",
      <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-WoS1_2-0">
       <a href="#cite_note-WoS1-2">
        [2]
       </a>
      </sup>
      first out of 88 journals in the category "Energy &amp; Fuels",
      <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-WoS2_3-0">
       <a href="#cite_note-WoS2-3">
        [3]
       </a>
      </sup>
      first out of 135 journals in the category "Engineering, Chemical",
      <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-WoS3_4-0">
       <a href="#cite_note-WoS3-4">
        [4]
       </a>
      </sup>
      and first among 225 journals in the category "Environmental Sciences".
      <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-WoS4_5-0">
       <a href="#cite_note-WoS4-5">
        [5]
       </a>
      </sup>
     </p>

     <h2>
      <span class="mw-headline" id="Article_types">
       Article types
      </span>
      <span class="mw-editsection">
       <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
        [
       </span>
       <a href="/w/index.php?title=Energy_and_Environmental_Science&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Article types">
        edit

       <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
        ]
       </span>
      </span>
     </h2>
     <p>
      <i>
       Energy &amp; Environmental Science
      </i>
      publishes the following types of articles: Research Papers (original scientific work); Review Articles, Perspectives, and Minireviews (feature review-type articles of broad interest); Communications (original scientific work of an urgent nature), Opinions (personal, often speculative, viewpoints or hypotheses on a current topic), and Analysis Articles (in-depth examination of energy and environmental technologies, strategies, policies, and general conceptual frameworks of general interest).
     </p>
