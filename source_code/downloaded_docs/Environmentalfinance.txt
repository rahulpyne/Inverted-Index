     <p>
      <b>
       Environmental finance
      </b>
      is the use of various
      <a href="/wiki/Financial_instrument" title="Financial instrument">
       financial instruments
      </a>
      (usually
      <a href="/wiki/Land_trust" title="Land trust">
       land trusts
      </a>
      and
      <a href="/wiki/Emissions_trading" title="Emissions trading">
       emissions trading
      </a>
      ) to protect the environment. The field is part of both
      <a href="/wiki/Environmental_economics" title="Environmental economics">
       environmental economics
      </a>
      and the
      <a href="/wiki/Conservation_movement" title="Conservation movement">
       conservation movement
      </a>
      .
     </p>
     <p>
      The field of Environmental Finance was first defined by
      <a href="/wiki/Richard_L._Sandor" title="Richard L. Sandor">
       Richard L. Sandor
      </a>
      , American economist and entrepreneur, when he taught the first ever Environmental Finance course at Columbia University in the fall of 1992.
     </p>
     <p>
      Dr. Gretchen Daily, of
      <a href="/wiki/Stanford_University" title="Stanford University">
       Stanford University
      </a>
      has written a book,
      <i>
       The New Economy of Nature
      </i>
      that addresses the issue of financing
      <a href="/wiki/Ecosystem_services" title="Ecosystem services">
       ecosystem services
      </a>
      .
     </p>
     <p>
      Dr. Jürg P. Blum, defined the term environmental finance (Dissertation: Corporate Environmental Responsibility and Corporate Economic Performance..... 1994 at
      <a href="/wiki/Alliant_International_University" title="Alliant International University">
       USIU
      </a>
      ) as a fairly new field, "concerned mainly with finance and investment regarding the ecological environment. The term environment, although frequently used in areas, such as strategic management (Ansoff, 1968), has been popularized throughout literature synonymously with the term ecological environment."
