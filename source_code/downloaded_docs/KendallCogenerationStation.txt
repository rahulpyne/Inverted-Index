     <p>
      The
      <b>
       Kendall Cogeneration Station
      </b>
      is a power station owned by
      <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Veolia_Energy_North_America&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Veolia Energy North America (page does not exist)">
       Veolia Energy North America
      </a>
      that produces both steam and power for the cities of
      <a href="/wiki/Boston" title="Boston">
       Boston
      </a>
      and
      <a href="/wiki/Cambridge,_Massachusetts" title="Cambridge, Massachusetts">
       Cambridge
      </a>
      .
     </p>
