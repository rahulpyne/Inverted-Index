   <h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">
    Quietrevolution wind turbine
   </h1>
   <div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">

     <p>
      The
      <b>
       quietrevolution
      </b>
      is a discontinued brand of (
      <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/VAWT" title="VAWT">
       VAWT
      </a>
      ) vertical axis helical turbine. The helical design is most similar to the
      <a href="/wiki/Gorlov_helical_turbine" title="Gorlov helical turbine">
       Gorlov helical turbine
      </a>
      . Both are an evolution of the
      <a href="/wiki/Darrieus_wind_turbine" title="Darrieus wind turbine">
       Darrieus wind turbine
      </a>
      . It has won several awards including the "Sustainable Innovation Award" in 2006.
     </p>
     <p>
      The turbine consists of three vertical
      <a href="/wiki/Airfoil" title="Airfoil">
       airfoil
      </a>
      blades, each having a
      <a href="/wiki/Helix" title="Helix">
       helical
      </a>
      twist of 120 degrees. This feature spreads the
      <a href="/wiki/Torque" title="Torque">
       torque
      </a>
      evenly over the entire revolution, thus preventing the destructive pulsations of the straight-bladed giromill (Darrieus turbine). The wind pushes each blade around on both the windward and
      <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Leeward" title="Leeward">
       leeward
      </a>
      sides of the turbine.
     </p>
     <p>
      The qr5 turbine, rated for 6 kW, measures 3.0m in diameter by 5m high.
     </p>
     <p>
      Poor performance of a QR5 installed at Welsh government offices in
      <a href="/wiki/Aberystwyth" title="Aberystwyth">
       Aberystwyth
      </a>
      has been blamed on poor siting.
      <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1">
       <a href="#cite_note-1">
        [1]
       </a>
      </sup>
      The turbine's overall cost was given as £48,000 and in 2012 it generated an average of 33
      <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/KWh" title="KWh">
       kWh
      </a>
      per month, a value of £5.28 per month.
     </p>
