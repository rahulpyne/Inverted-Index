     <p>
      The
      <b>
       Department of Climate Change and Energy Efficiency
      </b>
      was an
      <a href="/wiki/Australia" title="Australia">
       Australian
      </a>
      <a href="/wiki/Government_of_Australia" title="Government of Australia">
       Government
      </a>
      <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Government_department" title="Government department">
       department
      </a>
      . Its role was to serve the people of Australia by developing a more prosperous and sustainable Australia by leading and coordinating the mitigation of greenhouse gas emissions, promotion of energy efficiency,
      <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Adaptation_to_climate_change" title="Adaptation to climate change">
       adaptation to climate change
      </a>
      and shaping of global solutions.
     </p>
     <p>
      The Department was led by its Secretary, Blair Comley
      <a href="/wiki/Public_Service_Medal_(Australia)" title="Public Service Medal (Australia)">
       PSM
      </a>
      who was responsible to the
      <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Minister_for_Climate_Change_and_Energy_Efficiency_(Australia)" title="Minister for Climate Change and Energy Efficiency (Australia)">
       Minister for Climate Change and Energy Efficiency
      </a>
      , the Honourable
      <a href="/wiki/Greg_Combet" title="Greg Combet">
       Greg Combet
      </a>
      <a href="/wiki/Order_of_Australia" title="Order of Australia">
       AM
      </a>
      <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Member_of_Parliament#Australia" title="Member of Parliament">
       MP
      </a>
      . The minister was assisted by the Parliamentary Secretary for Climate Change and Energy Efficiency, the Honourable
      <a href="/wiki/Mark_Dreyfus" title="Mark Dreyfus">
       Mark Dreyfus
      </a>
      <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Queens_Counsel" title="Queens Counsel">
       QC
      </a>
      MP.
     </p>
     <p>
      On 25 March 2013, responsibility for Climate Change passed to the newly formed
      <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Industry,_Innovation,_Climate_Change,_Science,_Research_and_Tertiary_Education" title="Department of Industry, Innovation, Climate Change, Science, Research and Tertiary Education">
       Department of Industry, Innovation, Climate Change, Science, Research and Tertiary Education
      </a>
      .
      <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2">
       <a href="#cite_note-2">
        [2]
       </a>
      </sup>
      <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-NAA_CA_9310_1-2">
       <a href="#cite_note-NAA_CA_9310-1">
        [1]
       </a>
      </sup>
     </p>

     <h2>
      <span class="mw-headline" id="Mission_and_outcomes">
       Mission and outcomes
      </span>
      <span class="mw-editsection">
       <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
        [
       </span>
       <a href="/w/index.php?title=Department_of_Climate_Change_and_Energy_Efficiency&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Mission and outcomes">

     </h2>
     <p>
      The department dealt with:
      <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3">
       <a href="#cite_note-3">
        [3]
       </a>
      </sup>
     </p>
     <ul>
      <li>
       Development and co-ordination of domestic and international climate change policy
      </li>
      <li>
       International climate change negotiations
      </li>
      <li>
       Design and implementation of emissions trading
      </li>
      <li>
       Mandatory renewable energy target policy, regulation and co-ordination
      </li>
      <li>
       Greenhouse emissions and energy consumption reporting
      </li>
      <li>
       Climate change adaptation strategy and co-ordination
      </li>
      <li>
       Co-ordination of climate change science activities
      </li>
     </ul>
