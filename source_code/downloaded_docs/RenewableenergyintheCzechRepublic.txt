    <div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en">
     <p>
      <b>
       Renewable energy in the Czech Republic
      </b>
      describes the
      <a href="/wiki/Renewable_energy" title="Renewable energy">
       renewable energy
      </a>
      related development in the
      <a href="/wiki/Energy_in_the_Czech_Republic" title="Energy in the Czech Republic">
       Energy in the Czech Republic
      </a>
      .
     </p>
     <p>
      According to
      <a href="/wiki/Eurostat" title="Eurostat">
       Eurostat
      </a>
      ,
      <a href="/wiki/Renewable_energy" title="Renewable energy">
       renewables
      </a>
      share in the Czech Republic in 2009 was 5% of the
      <a href="/wiki/Energy_mix" title="Energy mix">
       energy mix
      </a>
      in total (Mtoe) and 6% of gross electricity generation (TWh). The energy consumption by fuel included in 2009: 40% coal, petroleum 21%, gas 15%, nuclear 16% and renewables 5%. Most electricity was produced with coal (55%) and nuclear (33%).
      <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1">
       <a href="#cite_note-1">
        [1]
       </a>
      </sup>
     </p>
